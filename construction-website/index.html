Our Services

We focus on the remodeling work that delivers the biggest daily‑living upgrade—planned tightly, built cleanly, and finished with shop‑grade precision. Custom fabrication is available when it adds meaningful value.

Kitchen Remodeling

Reconfigure flow & storage.

Layout refinements, improved work zones, lighting layers, durable surfaces, ventilation that actually clears steam & grease. Planning prevents mid‑project change orders; cabinet shop capability ensures tight fit at finish.

Selective Custom: Islands, range hoods, specialty pull‑outs, infills.

Bathroom Renovations

Moisture‑smart, comfort‑forward.

Waterproofed assemblies, efficient ventilation, heated options, precise tile layout, layered lighting. We balance material upgrades with long‑term maintenance ease.

Selective Custom: Vanities, recessed niches, linen towers.

Interior / Whole‑Home Updates

Unify flow & finishes.

Open sight lines, update flooring & trim, improve lighting circuits, integrate smart controls. Sequenced trades keep finish stages clean.

Selective Custom: Built‑ins, feature walls, beam wraps.

Additions & Structural Improvements

Seamless integration.

Engineering coordination, foundation & framing, mechanical tie‑ins, exterior detailing continuity so the addition feels original.

Selective Custom: Beam cladding, storage walls.

Selective Custom Cabinetry & Built‑Ins

Precision where it helps most.

In‑house fabrication solves awkward dimensions, maximizes storage, and accelerates schedule. Recommended when it matches or beats modified semi‑custom cost or adds real function.

Examples: Mudroom benches, media walls, pantries.
